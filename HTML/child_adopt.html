ARODHI

It functions as the nodal body for adoption of Indian and Foriegn children and is mandated to monitor and regulate in-country and inter-country adoptions. ARODHI is designated as the Central Authority to deal with inter-country adoptions in accordance with the provisions of the Hague Convention on Inter-country Adoption, 1993, ratified by Government of India in 2003. ARODHI primarily deals with adoption of orphan, abandoned and surrendered children through its associated /recognised adoption agencies.


Our main partners

 

 

Surakhsha

Surakhsha provides adoption, foster care, and pregnancy counseling services, as well as counseling to families, assist refugees, and immigrants resettling.

Christian children's society

Christian children's society is a faith-based humanitarian organization and adoption agency based in Eugene, Oregon, United States, known for international adoption and child welfare.

Ashrayaa

Ashrayaa is a registered, non-governmental organization which has been serving the community of underprivileged children and women in Karnataka for the past thirty three years.

Shrishti Mandir

Shrishti Mandir has been working quietly towards creating a generation of children who are not bound by the circumstances they were born into.
